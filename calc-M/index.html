Instructions

Basic Operations

Addition (+): Enter the first number, press '+', enter the second number, and press '=' to get the result.

Subtraction (−): Enter the first number, press '−', enter the second number, and press '=' to get the result.

Multiplication (×): Enter the first number, press '×', enter the second number, and press '=' to get the result.

Division (÷): Enter the first number, press '÷', enter the second number, and press '=' to get the result.

Advanced Functions

Square Root (√x): Press '√x', enter the number, and press '=' to get the square root.

Square (x²): Press 'x²', enter the number, and press '=' to get the square.

Power (xʸ): Press 'xʸ', enter the base number, press 'Next', enter the exponent, and press '=' to get the result.

Logarithm (logbx): Press 'logbx', enter the value (x), press 'Next', enter the base (b), and press '=' to get the logarithm.

Mean Absolute Deviation (MAD): Press 'MAD', enter the number of values, input each value followed by 'Next', and press '=' to calculate the MAD.

Standard Deviation (Std Dev): Press 'Std Dev', enter the number of values, input each value followed by 'Next', and press '=' to calculate the standard deviation.

Arccos: Press 'Arccos', enter side lengths a, b, and c, pressing 'Next' after each, and press '=' to calculate the angle.
